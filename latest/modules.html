Bases: Loss

Implementation of the Jaccard distance, or Intersection over Union IoU loss. Jaccard = (|X & Y|)/ (|X|+ |Y| - |X & Y|)

= sum(|A*B|)/(sum(|A|)+sum(|B|)-sum(|A*B|))

Implementation taken from https://github.com/keras-team/keras-contrib.git

Implementation of the Tanimoto distance, which is modified version of the Jaccard distance. Tanimoto = (|X & Y|)/ (|X|^2+ |Y|^2 - |X & Y|)

= sum(|A*B|)/(sum(|A|^2)+sum(|B|^2)-sum(|A*B|))

Implementation taken from https://github.com/feevos/resuneta
